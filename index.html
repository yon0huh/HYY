HOBBY

My hobby is watching the same movie several times. I don't have many favorite movie genres, so I watch interesting movies over and over again. I usually watch Disney animation or comedy movies. It's good to be able to focus on other things every time I watch a movie. If I watch the same movie several times, I can understand the movie better, and I can see the small details that I didn't see at first. For example, Olaf was so cute in Frozen 2 that I watched movies more than five times in the movie theater. I haven't seen many movies lately because I haven't been to the movie theater often, but I've seen the movie 'Soul' three times. If I watch a movie once and think it's fun, I watch it at least twice in the theater. I also make photo tickets for movies that I watched as a secondary hobby.
